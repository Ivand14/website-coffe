Café LUX* revolutionised the world of hotel coffee overnight, and our commitment remains clear: to offer guests the finest barista-made coffee, day or night. Due to its high demand and resounding success, LUX* resorts and hotels are expanding the Café LUX* concept outside our resorts through unique and exciting franchises.

The Café LUX* opened its doors at the Trianon Shopping Mall, Bagatelle Shopping Mall, Caudan Shopping Mall and the SSR airport of Mauritius.

Cool and trendy, it's a laid-back spot to catch up with friends and perfect for a light lunch with complimentary WiFi on the go, coupled with a focus on home-made fresh products.

CAFE LUX FRANCHISE

We believe that great coffee isn't just a luxury; it's a daily essential

With Café LUX* in operation within 5 of our resorts, we are now branching out and opened our first Café LUX* franchise at the Trianon Shopping Mall, Mauritius with a second due to open at Bagatelle Shopping Mall, Mauritius by the end of this of 2015. Cool and trendy, it's a laid-back spot to catch up with friends and perfect for a light lunch with a focus on home-made fresh products. We're there for support with all new Café LUX* franchises, and to ensure that your customers will always enjoy the passion, quality and stylish ambience of the authentic Café LUX*. Reflecting our commitment to innovation, our specialist baristas keep up to date with all the latest trends and techniques through training including Cold Press, Pour Overs and Nitro coffee.
