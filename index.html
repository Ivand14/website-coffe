THE SMELL OF BEANS BEING ROASTED ENCOURAGES JOYFUL FEELINGS

Café LUX* revolutionised the world of hotel coffee overnight, and our commitment remains clear: to offer guests the finest barista-made coffee, day or night. Due to its high demand and resounding success, LUX* resorts and hotels are expanding the Café LUX* concept outside our resorts through unique and exciting franchises.

The Café LUX* opened its doors at the Trianon Shopping Mall, Bagatelle Shopping Mall, Caudan Shopping Mall and the SSR airport of Mauritius.

Cool and trendy, it's a laid-back spot to catch up with friends and perfect for a light lunch with complimentary WiFi on the go, coupled with a focus on home-made fresh products.

TOP 5 REASONS TO DRINK COFFE

Phillipa Rowney, a South African blogger, is a true coffee lover! Her blog, Coffey and Cake, explores the delicious world of coffee, cafes, sweet treats… She shares with us her Top 5 reasons to drink coffee:

There is nothing like the delicious smell of brewing coffee- the roasting beans give off the most enticing and comforting smell that make me fill up with joy instantly.

When you buy coffee your car will smell amazing for days- this I realised after Paul [Phillipa's husband] and I left our bag of coffee in the car for a day and it was the most incredible result... coffee flavoured air freshener.

If you need to stay awake have a shot of coffee- we all have those nights were sleep is a no go and work trumps DJ Pillow and Club Duvet. Coffee will be your best friend on these occasions.

It is a great way to connect with friends- This is the extrovert coming out in me, but what better way then to catch up with a friend over a good cup of coffee. It is my favourite past time. Great company, great coffee and my heart is content for days.

It aids digestion- it's true. Coffee has a chemical in it that assists in digestion. I must say this natural remedy is far more appealing to me then what the doctor or chemist may prescribe.
